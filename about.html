Our story

Dallas's Premier Carpentry Experts

Our mission is to provide Dallas homeowners with exceptional craftsmanship, honest communication, and lasting value. We believe every project deserves the highest standard of quality.

Thunderstorm Roofing & Construction was founded in 2010 by a team of master carpenters who saw a need for reliable, high-quality carpentry in Dallas. Starting with small trim jobs, our reputation for precision and integrity quickly grew. Today, we handle everything from custom decks to full home renovations, always with the same hands-on approach.

We are proud to be a family-owned business serving the Dallas community. Our team stays up-to-date with the latest techniques and materials, but we never lose sight of the fundamentals: careful measurement, solid joinery, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. When you hire Thunderstorm, you're getting more than a contractor – you're getting a partner who cares about your home as much as you do.
