Our story

Built on Quality, Driven by Craft

To deliver exceptional carpentry that enhances homes and lives through meticulous craftsmanship, honest communication, and unwavering integrity.

Thunderstorm Roofing & Construction was founded in 2010 by a master carpenter with a passion for building. What started as a small crew doing roof repairs quickly grew into a full-service carpentry company serving the Dallas metro area. Our founder believed that every project—whether a tiny trim repair or a custom deck—deserves the same level of care and precision. Over the years, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and attention to detail that keeps our customers coming back.

Today, we are a team of skilled carpenters who take pride in our work. We stay current with the latest techniques and materials, but our core values remain the same: do it right the first time, treat every home like our own, and never cut corners. From framing to finish, we are committed to crafting spaces that our clients love for years to come.
