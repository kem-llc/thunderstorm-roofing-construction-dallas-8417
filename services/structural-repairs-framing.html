Structural Repairs & Framing

Expert repair of rotted beams, sagging floors, and damaged framing to restore safety and stability.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our structural carpentry services address issues like rotted sill plates, termite-damaged studs, sagging floor joists, and compromised roof rafters. We assess the damage, engineer solutions, and replace or reinforce affected members with code-compliant materials. Our work ensures your home remains safe and structurally sound. We also handle new construction framing for additions, garages, and interior walls. We coordinate with engineers and inspectors as needed to guarantee quality and compliance.

What's included

  • Restore structural integrity and safety
  • Prevent further damage and costly repairs
  • Code-compliant work with permits
  • Experienced with insurance claims and inspections

How it works

1

Inspection & Assessment

We inspect the damage, identify the cause, and provide a detailed repair plan.

2

Permitting & Engineering

If required, we obtain permits and work with structural engineers to design repairs.

3

Repair & Reinforcement

We remove damaged materials and install new framing, ensuring proper load paths and connections.

Common questions

How do I know if I have structural damage?

Common signs include sagging floors, cracks in walls, doors that stick, and visible rot. We offer free inspections to assess.

Will you work with my insurance company?

Yes, we can provide documentation and estimates to support your claim and coordinate with adjusters.
