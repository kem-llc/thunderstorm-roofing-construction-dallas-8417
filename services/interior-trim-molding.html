Interior Trim & Molding

Add elegance and character with custom baseboards, crown molding, wainscoting, and more.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our interior trim services transform ordinary rooms into refined spaces. We install baseboards, door casings, window trim, crown molding, chair rails, and wainscoting with precise mitered corners and seamless joints. We also create custom built-in shelving, bookcases, and entertainment centers that maximize storage and style. Our carpenters match existing trim profiles or design new ones to complement your home's architecture. We use primed finger-jointed pine, MDF, or solid wood to suit your preference and budget.

What's included

  • Enhance room aesthetics and resale value
  • Seamless integration with existing trim
  • Durable, smooth finishes ready for paint
  • Custom built-ins for added functionality
