Custom Deck Building

Design and build your dream outdoor deck with premium materials and expert craftsmanship.

Schedule this service

Overview

We specialize in creating custom decks that enhance your home's living space and value. From simple platforms to multi-level designs with built-in seating, planters, and lighting, our team handles every detail. We work with pressure-treated lumber, cedar, tropical hardwoods, and composite materials to match your style and budget. Each deck is engineered for safety and durability, with proper footings, joist spacing, and fasteners. We also manage permits and inspections so you can relax and enjoy the result.

What's included

  • Increase home value and curb appeal
  • Custom designs tailored to your property
  • Durable, low-maintenance materials
  • Professional project management from start to finish

How it works

1

Consultation & Design

We meet on-site to discuss your vision, take measurements, and create a detailed plan with material options and cost estimates.

2

Permitting & Preparation

We handle all necessary permits and prepare the site, including leveling and laying foundations.

3

Construction & Finish

Our skilled carpenters build your deck with precision, applying stain or sealant for lasting beauty.

Common questions

How long does it take to build a deck?

Most residential decks take 1-2 weeks, depending on size and complexity. We provide a timeline during the consultation.

What decking material do you recommend?

It depends on your budget and maintenance preferences. Composite decking requires less upkeep, while wood offers a classic look. We'll help you choose.
